Hello!

My name is Bethany, and this is my E-Portfolio of my experience here at USC! My time at USC has provided me with so many opportunities that I would have otherwise not gotten. I came in undeclared and managed to work my way to becoming a Biomedical Engineering major, something I would not have imagined myself ever doing. I applied for a job to be a Spanish tutor and was given the opportunity to instead be a calculus SI, which involved knowledge of math and speaking to large groups, two things I had never thought I would enjoy doing. This ended up giving me a love of calculus and the ability to speak to a room and facilitate activities to help others better appreciate math. I was then given the opportunity to develop Android applications for research purposes, when I had never programmed anything in my life. I ended up falling in love with Computer Science and research as a whole, encouraging me to choose a Graduation with Leadership Distinction with a research pathway.

This portfolio will review the insights I have had during my time at USC and how they have changed my perspective towards research. It also will go into my leadership plan where I discuss how I will use these insights after my time here at USC.
